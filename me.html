Hi I am Famin

I am an immigrant from Bangladesh. I landed at John F. Kennedy Airport(JFK), New York City, when I immigrated to the US. Interestingly, a few years after we got to NYC, my family and I moved next to JFK. So, so my daily life consists of hearing airplanes flying off to the world while doing homework or chores. When I go out side, I can see airplanes and lights from JFK. So, JFK is largely part of my New York and my identity as an immigrant.
